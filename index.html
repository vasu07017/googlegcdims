About Us

Welcome to the IMSEC GDG! We are a community of tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators at IMS Engineering College. Join us to learn, build, and grow together in the world of technology.The GDG on Campus - IMSEC chapter is a vibrant student-led community focused on fostering a collaborative environment for tech enthusiasts. It aims to create a culture where students can discuss projects, explore new tech stacks, and support each other in learning. Through workshops, events, and hands-on sessions, the chapter encourages innovation, peer-to-peer learning, and personal growth, helping students stay at the forefront of technology.
